Richard Stallman

Character

Richard Stallman is a programmer - quite an astonashing one aswell. But, beyond that, he is a political activist; he is a campaigner of human rights with regards to software - a field not typically considered when reflecting upon our liberties. He is the architect of the GNU project and a front runner in the free software movement. Stallman argues for a world without the proprietary software that lets coorparations puppet us with malicious programs that strip control away from the user.

The free software movement, and subsidiaries of, has been covered already but the crucial takeaway here is that Richard Stallman was behind it; those that continue to fight the companies we mindlessly sell our souls to are doing so because of him. Fame frequently corrupts those that aquire it but if anyone is impervious to any form of corruption, or even moral ambiguity, it's Richard Stallman.

Linus Torvalds

Character

Linus Torvalds is most famously known for developing the Linux kernal as well as Git. The linux kernal is present in most of the worlds computers. From desktops to smartphones to fridges, Linux is everywhere. It serves as the kernal for most GNU/Linux operating systems aswell as the world's largest mobile OS: Android.

Linus originally began writing the kernal as he was displeased by the costs assosiated with pre-existing UNIX and MS-DOS systems. After completion of the kenral, those at the GNU project, who needed a kernal to complete their operating system, decided to use it and so GNU/Linux was born.
